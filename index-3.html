How We Operate

Our Principles

UK-Based Operations

SwitcherMate operates from the United Kingdom. Our business practices, agreements, and client relationships are governed by and conducted under UK regulatory frameworks.

Compliance-Led Approach

We maintain a compliance-led approach across all operational areas. Partner relationships are subject to due diligence and ongoing oversight to uphold agreed service standards.

Data Protection & Transparency

We operate in accordance with UK GDPR. Personal and business data is handled with care, used only for legitimate service delivery purposes, and is never sold or shared with unauthorised third parties.

Long-Term Partnerships

We prioritise enduring, accountable relationships with clients and partners over transactional engagements. Our value is built through consistency, reliability, and clear communication over time.
